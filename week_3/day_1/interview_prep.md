# Norms Considerations

Discuss these with your group before proceeding!

- Most importantly, how can you be supportive?
- In some coding interviews, interviewers will occasionally speak or ask questions. Do you want this? If so, when/what kind?
- You will probably get stuck at some point during the problem. What should your group do? (Remember, some amount of struggle is productive.)
- How will you manage time for interviews and feedback?
- Different people will have different answers!

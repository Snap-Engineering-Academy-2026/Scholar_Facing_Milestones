# Reminder
Discuss your interview norms with your team at the start of your sessions. Right before you start your mock interview, remind your team what you are looking for in terms of help and support. 
1. How would you like to be supported? (this can also mean asking for your peers to be hands-off until explicitly asked!)
2. In some coding interviews, interviewers will occasionally speak or ask questions. Do you want this? If so, when/what kind?
3. You will probably get stuck at some point during the problem. What should your group do? (Remember, some amount of struggle is productive.)
4. How will you manage time for interviews and feedback?

Different people will have different answers!
